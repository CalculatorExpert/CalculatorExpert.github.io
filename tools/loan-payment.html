How it Works

Calculates the fully amortized monthly payment, or reverses the equation to find your max loan amount or required term length.

Formula

PMT = [P * r * (1+r)^n] / [(1+r)^n - 1]

Frequently Asked Questions

What is an amortization schedule?

It's a table showing each periodic payment on an amortizing loan, including how much goes to interest and how much to principal.

How does interest rate affect my payment?

Higher interest rates lead to higher monthly payments and more total interest paid over the life of the loan.

Can I solve for the loan term?

Yes, our calculator allows you to input your desired payment and see how many months it will take to pay off the loan.
