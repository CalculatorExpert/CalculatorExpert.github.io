Frequently Asked Questions

How many billable hours are in a week?

Most freelancers find 20-30 hours billable, with the rest spent on admin and marketing.

Should I include taxes in my rate?

Yes! Freelancers must pay both portions of FICA taxes, so add a 25-30% buffer for taxes.

How do I account for time off?

Calculate your hourly rate based on 48 weeks instead of 52 to allow for vacation and sick days.
