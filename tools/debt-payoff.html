Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Debt Avalanche?

A strategy where you pay off debts with the highest interest rates first to minimize total interest paid.

What is the Debt Snowball?

A strategy where you pay off the smallest balances first to gain momentum and psychological wins.

Should I pay off debt or save?

Generally, pay off high-interest debt (over 7%) before aggressive saving, but keep a small emergency fund first.
