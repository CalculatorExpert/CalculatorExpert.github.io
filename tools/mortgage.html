Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a monthly mortgage payment?

A standard PITI payment includes Principal, Interest, Taxes, and Insurance.

How much down payment do I need?

While 20% is traditional, many loans allow as little as 3% or even 0% for specific programs.

What is PMI?

Private Mortgage Insurance is usually required if you have a down payment less than 20% of the home's value.
