Frequently Asked Questions

What is compound interest?

Compound interest is the interest on a loan or deposit calculated based on both the initial principal and the accumulated interest from previous periods.

How often can interest be compounded?

Typically, interest is compounded daily, monthly, quarterly, or annually. Our tool assumes monthly compounding for contributions.

What's the difference between simple and compound interest?

Simple interest is only calculated on the principal amount, while compound interest includes interest on interest.
