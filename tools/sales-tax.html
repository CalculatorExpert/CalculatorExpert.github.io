Frequently Asked Questions

How do I extract tax from a total?

Divide the total by (1 + TaxRate). For example, at 10% tax, divide by 1.10.

What is VAT?

Value Added Tax is a consumption tax placed on a product whenever value is added at each stage of the supply chain.

Is sales tax the same everywhere?

No, it varies significantly by state, city, and country.
