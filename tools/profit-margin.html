How it Works

Calculates Gross Profit (Revenue - COGS) and Net Profit (Gross - Expenses), displayed as percentages.

Formula

Margin = (Revenue - Expenses) / Revenue

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a good profit margin?

It varies by industry, but a net margin of 10% is considered average, while 20% is high.

What is Gross Margin?

The percentage of revenue exceeding the Cost of Goods Sold.

How is Net Margin different?

Net Margin subtracts all expenses, including taxes and operating costs, not just production costs.
