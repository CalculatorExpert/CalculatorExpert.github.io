Frequently Asked Questions

What is the average inflation rate?

Historically, the U.S. average has been around 3% annually, though it can vary significantly by year.

How does inflation hurt savers?

Inflation reduces the purchasing power of your money, meaning your savings will buy fewer goods and services in the future.

What assets protect against inflation?

Equities, real estate, and inflation-protected bonds (like TIPS) are common hedges.
