Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set a realistic savings goal?

Evaluate your income and expenses to see what you can comfortably set aside each month, then use this tool to see how long it takes.

Should I save or invest for my goal?

Short-term goals (under 3 years) are usually better for high-yield savings. Long-term goals can benefit from market investments.

What is the 52-week challenge?

It's a popular savings method where you save an increasing amount each week, though our tool focuses on consistent monthly contributions.
