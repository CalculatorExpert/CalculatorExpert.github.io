How it Works

Divides fixed costs by the contribution margin (price minus variable cost).

Formula

Units = Fixed Costs / (Price - Variable Cost)

Frequently Asked Questions

What are fixed costs?

Expenses that do not change with sales volume, like rent or salaries.

What are variable costs?

Costs that increase directly with production, like raw materials or shipping.

How can I lower my break-even point?

By reducing fixed costs, lowering variable costs, or increasing the selling price.
