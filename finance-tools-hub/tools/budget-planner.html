How it Works

Splits take-home pay into Needs (50%), Wants (30%), and Savings/Debt (20%).

Formula

Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a 'Need'?

Housing, utilities, groceries, transportation, and minimum debt payments.

What counts as a 'Want'?

Dining out, entertainment, hobbies, and non-essential travel.

What if my needs are more than 50%?

You may need to reduce wants or search for ways to lower fix costs like housing or insurance.
